Mission

We specialise in the creative design and construction of luxury new homes, duplexes, townhouses and apartments, as well as custom fit outs for commercial premises.Our clients are individuals, developers and businesses who need premium quality design and construction. We provide our valued clients with the highly personalised and professional service that they deserve.Our project experience includes the design of high-end residential homes, medium-density multi-residential developments, and commercial premises such as restaurants, offices and clinics. All of our projects are delivered on (or before) schedule by our highly skilled and licensed staff. We take great pride in the high quality of our work and in ensuring total client satisfaction.

Building Expertise

We have 10+ years of local experience working on a range of project scopes-no job is too big or small. From residential renovation & extension, knockdown rebuild house, to duplex and townhouses. We always focused on client expectations. With solid project management experience, we committed to providing design and construction services that focus on the three interrelated keys of Scope, Schedule and Cost. All decisions take into consideration these three objectives, which ensures a quality project is delivered on time and within budget.
